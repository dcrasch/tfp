How to use

  1. Download the FigureDB.pdb from your palm. (howto see the Gallery)
  2. Unzip pytessel-0.1.0.tar.gz (unix: tar xvzf pytessel-0.1.0.tar.gz)
  3. Place the FigureDB.pdb in the pytessel directory.
  4. Start the pytessel.py program:
    • python2.2 pytessel.py (generates all the figures)
    • python2.2 pytessel.py 3 (generates the third figure)
    • python2.2 pytessel.py 7-10 (generate the 7th through 10th figure)
  5. wait ....
  6. the images are placed in the pytessel directory
